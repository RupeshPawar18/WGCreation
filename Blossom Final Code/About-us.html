About Us
GENERAL EDUCATION INSTITUTE | BLOSSOM SCHOOL

A New Dimension to Learning

The General Education Institute, owes its existence to the commendable efforts of Late Gopal Narayan Askikar and Late Ganesh Ramchandra Lele. The institute established in the year 1892( 24th April 1892). The institute of present is conducting 8 Pre-primary Schools, 8 Primary schools, 11 Secondary Marathi Medium, 7 Junior colleges, 2 Pre-primary and Primary Schools, 1 Secondary English Medium, 3 CBSE Schools and 1 Senior College in Mumbai, Thane and Raigad Districts of Maharashtra. The Institute is proud that almost all Secondary Schools are oldest or 1st in the respective cities.


No cause greater than the cause of Education in which the General Education Institute have distinguished themselves for establishing schools in Remote corners of the state.>


The General Education Institute which has been engaged in the noble cause of education had to surmount various difficulties and obstacles in knitting the various loose threads to create in a finest fabric of its school complexes.

The General Education Institute in 21st century have decided to undertake the following major projects for the benefits of citizens of Dombivli, Kalyan, Bhivandi, Uran, Thane, Kurla, Dadar and surrounding areas where the institute is conducting its schools.

  • To construct new Building for Blossom CBSE School, Dombivli.
  • To construct a new building and educational hub at L.P. Girls High School, Dadar.
  • To open English Medium Pre-Primary / Primary Schools at various school complexes which is the need of hour.
  • To open Senior College.
  • To repair and renovate Chhabildas High School, Dadar.
  • To repair and renovate New High School, Kalyan.
  • To Develop playground of N.I. Uran High School.

Blossom CBSE School is one of the school established and administered by the General Education Institute, Dadar. School is a well known for its unique & pioneering approach to education. It aims at providing excellence in education and proper concentration of fact thereby imbibing perfection , literacy, moral value, technical intricacies and physical development irrespective of religion, race, language or any other discrepancy.

We also pride ourselves on providing an excellent education that helps prepare young minds for the future. The school has a unique atmosphere of support & respect, which helps cultivate the ability & talent of our young people. Students and staff have created an open, friendly, flexible, yet rigorous approach to study. Our motto is "Learning By Doing" through which we offer an educational program completely based on creative, independent doing and thinking.
